Ideal Client Profile

LLMx serves high-agency, semi-technical operators with business context and urgency.

  • PMs, consultants, agency owners, founders, marketers, creators.
  • Comfortable with ChatGPT/Claude and AI prototyping tools.
  • Blocked by implementation friction, not by motivation.
  • Willing to pay because speed and leverage matter.
Where They Get Stuck

Can Usually

  • Describe product flows clearly.
  • Generate and edit AI-assisted code.
  • Move quickly in Cursor, Replit, v0, Lovable, or Bolt.

Cannot Consistently

  • Structure repos and Git workflows.
  • Debug local/cloud environment failures.
  • Deploy reliably and iterate without getting blocked.
